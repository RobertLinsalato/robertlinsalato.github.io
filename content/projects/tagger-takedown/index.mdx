## About the Game
You play as Gus, the always-tired custodian at an office in the middle of nowhere. You were RUDLY awaken by your boss telling you to come in late and deal with some hooligans graffitiing up the place. Armed with your trusty water gun and sentient mop bucket, will you get the job done?

## My Role
With Tagger Takedown, I got to work more on my level design skills. I tried to take the prompt "make an FPS style game" and be as creative as I could with it.
