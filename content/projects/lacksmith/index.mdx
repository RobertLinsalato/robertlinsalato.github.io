# About the Game
Create your weapons and earn your coin in the never-ending loop of smithery!

lacksmith is a VR simulation game where you play as a blacksmith in the woods.

# My Role
On "lacksmith", I dived deeper into Unreal Engine's Blueprint system. than I ever had. I was responsible for connecting all the gameplay systems together, programming the smithing portion, and modeling assets like the sword.
