## About the Game
Ezra the Guardian Angel was exploring Earth, trying to find their assignee, when all of a sudden a demon comes from nowhere and kicks them down to the pit of the earth. You must help Ezra get back to heaven to reconnect him to his person!

## My Role
With Heavenbound, I had the goal to create each art asset by myself... which didn't fully pan out. While a lot of it was done by yours truly (including both Ezra and the main enemy in the game), it became too much work to complete within the time frame. I'm still happy about the results as it was my first time wokring in 2D and it was a smooth and fun expereince.
