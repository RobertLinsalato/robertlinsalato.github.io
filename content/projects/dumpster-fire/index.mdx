# About the Game
An outbreak of fires have erupted all over Ferne Falls and it's up to a ragtag team of critters to put them out before it’s too late!

Dumpster Fire is a split screen co-op game where players put out house fires that were started by reckless and avoidable behaviors. Work together to solve puzzles and extinguish the flames as fast as possible!

# My Role
For Dumpster Fire, I served as Producer, 3D Artist, Programmer, Technical Artist, and Audio Engineer. I was able to create and rig both of the main characters along with making many props for the game.
