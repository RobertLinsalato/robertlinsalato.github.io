Knightfall follows wannabe knight, Oddie, on a quest to save the princess of his land. During this quest, the player will control Oddie as he runs, jumps, and slashes threats unlike ones he has ever faced. Our hero, Oddie, encounters entities that aren’t-so-one-sided and sees a hidden side of the world he had no idea could exist. How will a world where his future is decided change when he gains full control of his actions? Oddie finds that his world is not only more than it seems, but that there are people, creatures, and circumstances beyond the 2D world he thought he knew.

For Knightfall, I modeled, UV'd, and rigged both the main character, Oddie, and the Antagonist, Cassandra.
