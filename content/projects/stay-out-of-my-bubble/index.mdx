## About the Game
No one likes being around people too much, be careful though, because if Terry's too close... he'll explode!

## My Role
_Stay Out Of My Bubble_ was created during Global Game Jam 2025. It was my first (and still only) solo game jam and I went into the project, wanting to do as much as possible by myself as a way to push and test my skills as a developer. The final product (while basic), turned out better than I was expecting and I'm happy with how much I did in 48 hours.
