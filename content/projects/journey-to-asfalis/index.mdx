## About the Game
Embark on a Journey to Asfalìs bringing gifts to be the first to enter the tower which will protect you from the dangers of the treacherous environments. On the way, adventurers must face challenges while managing their inventory to overcome obstacles and setbacks.

## My Role
On _Journey to Asfalìs_, I helped design the game, sculpt, and 3D print the character pieces.
