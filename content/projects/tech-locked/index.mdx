## About the Game
You wake up after a weeks long journey traveling across the ocean to the lost island of mad inventor Harlow who built this island to escape from all the people trying to hold him back from greatness. With no one knowing what happened to Harlow, you are tasked with exploring the mountain and trying to understand what happened on this fateful island.

## My Role
_TECH___LOCKED_ was the first game I created at Azusa Pacific. The prompt was "create a 3D adventure game" and the rest was up to me.
