## About the Game
_Drama Happens_ is a 3D puzzle adventure game wherein the protagonist- that is, you the player- must navigate the Opera house to tidy up the lobby and do last-minute fixes backstage before the opening night of the big show. Time is limited, so each task must be completed before moving on to the next one and get out before everyone else takes the stage. The entire production depends on you, so break a leg!

## My Role
For _Drama Happens_, I was tasked with the 3D Modeling & Animation, Environment Design, & Programming. The game loop was programmed by myself, along with aiding in the design of the level.
