## About the Game
You are Pitahaya, a small mushroom creature tasked with restoring life back to the forest. The only way this can be done is through embarking on a trek ascending a turbulent hill while collecting 4 acorns to bring to the great tree. 

## My Role
Pitahaya was my first game jam ever. Created during Global Game Jam 2023, my team and I came up with the idea with the theme of "roots", the character 'rooting' themselves into the ground to protect themselves from wind. I modeled and rigged the character for the game, along with building the final export.
